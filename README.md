# Orange-Weather

A weather app for iOS 13+

This app does not store, share, or sell your location or any other personal information.  Weather at your current location is accessed via Apple's [CoreLocation](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/corelocation) service. This service does not make your information known to the developer.
